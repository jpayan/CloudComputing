2. List the 3 main things you need to do in order achieve your professional goals.
  • Overcoming setbacks. Life is not always as pleasant as we wish it was, nonetheless there is always something good to live for. Bad experiences happen to everyone, and even though we might think that the worst thing ever has happened to us, there are people that are going through or have gone through something just as severe. To do so, as mentioned by Sheryl Sandberg in her book Option B (2017), we must defeat and forget about the three P's:
    1. Personalization: "It's all my fault."
    2. Pervasiveness: "Because of this, I'm affecting everything and everyone around me."
    3. Permanence: "This will never get better."
  • Focusing on tomorrow without losing track of today. It is not wise to adventure to the real world without a plan, but nothing will workout if we don't pay attention to today. It simply doesn't make sense. If we worry too much about tomorrow, we tend to care less about today, and with so we forget that it is in the today when we get ready for tomorrow.
  • Asking for help. And this one is more personal, enough reason why I will write this paragraph in first person. I usually try to prove to myself that I can get through this by myself. That I don't need any special treatment, that achieving something with help is not as satisfying as achieving it by myself, but it is time for me to mature. I have to grow up and accept that I am not unbreakable, that I don't know everything and that I need help.
